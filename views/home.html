Check Your Tone

Consider this ...
* 2.4 million emails are sent every second.
* That means that there are 205 billion emails sent everyday.
* That's 74 trillion every year!

Now consider this ...
In a study conducted by the Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, email recipients
misinterpreted the tone of an email 50% of the time. Further, these recipients believed they
correctly interrupted the email 90% of the time. This means that almost 1.1 emails every second
are being misinterpreted even though the receiver thinks they interrupted it correctly.

So why does this matter:
All of us are walking around confused, unsure and completely ambivalent of what we
should be doing, thinking or feeling based on the emails we are sending and receiving
causing much misdirection, angst, hard feelings, doubt, misguided direction and ultimately
a loss of productivity in are personal and professional lives. At Check Your Tone, we believe
this is wrong and we want to help. We invite you to upload your email, social posts, letters, memos
